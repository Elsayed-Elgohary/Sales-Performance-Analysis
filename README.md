# 💼 Sales Data Analysis Project

## 🧠 Project Overview
A data analysis project focused on exploring sales data to identify trends, evaluate performance, and generate actionable business insights to support decision-making.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
The sales dataset contained large volumes of transaction data that needed analysis to understand business performance, customer behavior, and product profitability.

---

## ⚙️ Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance  
- Identify trends and patterns in revenue  
- Evaluate product and category performance  
- Support data-driven business decisions  

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation
- Cleaned and structured raw sales data  
- Handled missing values and inconsistencies  
- Standardized data formats  
- Prepared dataset for analysis  

---

## 📊 Analysis & Key Insights
- Identified top-performing products and categories  
- Analyzed revenue trends over time  
- Detected peak sales periods  
- Evaluated customer purchasing behavior  

---

## 📈 Key Findings
- Certain products contributed most of the revenue  
- Sales showed clear seasonal trends  
- Customer behavior varied across time periods  
- Some categories had higher profitability than others  

---

## 💡 Business Value
- Improved understanding of sales performance  
- Helped optimize product strategy  
- Supported better forecasting and planning  
- Enabled data-driven decision making
